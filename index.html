Know how you can also be part of CSI

Are you capable....

After a period of two years the commitee of CSI of our chapter organize an entrance test for those who are interested and find themselves capable enough to be part of one of the amazing body of technology.

Most of the times this test is divided into two parts:

  1. In the first half, enthusiastic students have to clear the pen-paper test in order to go for second round
  2. After clearing first round, one has to face Personal Interview where the candidates are asked several questions and they are tested on basis of their technical and analytical knowledge

The student who are able to clear both the rounds gets this amazing chance to become COMMITTE MEMBER of CSI
